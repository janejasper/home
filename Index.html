<h1>Jane Jasper</h1>

<h2>Personal Stylist</h2>

<body>

After studying at the prestigious London College of Fashion, Jane Jasper cut her teeth on titles such as iD and Dazed and Confused in London before returning to work at Australian Vogue. In addition to her editorial work, she has years of buying experience, having worked at Ksubi and Belinda before venturing out on her own. She owned and operated cult boutique Land’s End Store for 6 years, representing international top-end brands like Celine, Proenza Schouler and Balmain - many exclusively - making Lands End the destination for fashion-forward luxury. Seeking more flexibility than a demanding retail schedule allowed, and seeing a gap in the market for consumer based personal styling, with her new enterprise Jasper aims to help her clients build smartly chosen wardrobes tailored to their individual style.

Known for introducing directional pieces into a timeless look, she has been regularly featured in journals such as Harper’s Bazaar, Vogue and the Sydney Morning Herald. She was named one of the best dressed women in Australia by the Australian newspaper.
